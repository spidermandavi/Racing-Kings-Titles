Introduction

What is Racing Kings?

Racing Kings is a unique chess variant where the objective is to race your king to the eighth rank before your opponent. There is no check or checkmate, creating a completely different strategic experience.

Objective

Reach the Finish Line

Bring your king to the eighth rank before your opponent.

Strategy

No Checkmate

Planning and timing replace many traditional chess concepts.

Play
