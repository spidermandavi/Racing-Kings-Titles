Title Requirements

Official requirements for obtaining each title.

RKSGM ▼
  • 5000+ rated RK games
  • Reached 2500 rating
  • 2 rated tournament wins (10+ games, 2450+ performance)
  • 5 tournament performances (10+ games, 2450+ performance)
  • 1 RK Shield victory
RKGM ▼
  • 3000+ rated RK games
  • Reached 2400 rating
  • 1 rated tournament win (10+ games, 2400+ performance)
  • 3 tournament performances (10+ games, 2400+ performance)
RKIM ▼
  • 2000+ rated RK games
  • Reached 2300 rating
  • 3 rated tournament wins (10+ games, 2300+ performance)
RKM ▼
  • 1000+ rated RK games
  • Reached 2200 rating
  • 3 rated tournament wins (10+ games, 2200+ performance)
RKCM ▼
  • 500+ rated RK games
  • Reached 2100 rating
  • 3 rated tournament wins (10+ games, 2100+ performance)
RKWC ▼
  • Win at least one Racing Kings World Championship.
RKV ▼
  • 10000+ rated RK games
  • OR
  • 5+ years playing Racing Kings
RKHM ▼
  • Exceptional contribution to the Racing Kings community.
  • Must be officially recognized.
